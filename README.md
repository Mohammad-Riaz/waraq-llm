# WaraqLLM — Arabic Fusha Research Assistant
### مساعد البحث العربي

An AI-powered research and learning tool built for advanced Arabic (Fusha) learners. Upload Arabic documents, chat with an AI assistant about the content, and master vocabulary through flashcards and visual mind maps.

---

## The Problem It Solves

I'm a Pakistani expat student living in Saudi Arabia, passionate about learning advanced Arabic (Fusha). The challenge? Most AI tools are built for English speakers. There's no dedicated research assistant for someone trying to read classical Arabic texts, understand Fusha vocabulary in context, and actually track what they're learning.

WaraqLLM fixes that.

---

## Features

- **Multi-Document Upload** — Upload Arabic PDFs and research texts
- **AI Chat Assistant** — Ask questions about your documents with source citations
- **Flashcard System** — AI auto-generates Fusha vocabulary cards from your documents; track what you know
- **Mind Map View** — Visual concept map of document topics with progress tracking
- **Progress Tracker** — See how many words you've mastered across sessions

---

## How to Use

1. Open the app and paste your Gemini API key (top right) — wait for green ✅
2. Upload any Arabic PDF from the left sidebar
3. Ask the assistant questions in the right panel
4. Switch to **Flashcards** to learn vocabulary extracted from your documents
5. Switch to **Mind Map** to visualize and track key concepts
